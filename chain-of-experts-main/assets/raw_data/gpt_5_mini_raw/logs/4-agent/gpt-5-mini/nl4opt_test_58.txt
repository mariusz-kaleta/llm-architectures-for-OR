Analytical simplification
- Because each pill adds positive time, any optimal solution will not use more pills than required: C + V = 130 at optimum.
- Vitamin D pills are more expensive in time (6 > 5), so the objective is minimized by taking as few V as allowed, subject to the C ≥ V + 1 constraint.
- With C = 130 − V and C ≥ V + 1 ⇒ 130 − V ≥ V + 1 ⇒ 129 ≥ 2V ⇒ V ≤ 64.5 ⇒ V ≤ 64 (integer). Combined with V ≥ 40, the best choice is V = 40.
- Then C = 130 − 40 = 90 (which satisfies C ≥ V + 1 since 90 ≥ 41).

- C* = 90 calcium pills
- V* = 40 vitamin D pills
- Total time Z* = 5·90 + 6·40 = 450 + 240 = 690 minutes
