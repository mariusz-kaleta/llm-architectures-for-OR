[MODELER]:
**1. Decision variables**

Let  
- \(x \ge 0\): dollars invested in condos  
- \(y \ge 0\): dollars invested in detached houses  

**2. Objective function**

Each dollar in condos yields \$0.50 profit, and each dollar in detached houses yields \$1 profit.  
Maximize total profit:

\[
\max \; Z = 0.5x + 1.0y
\]

**3. Constraints**

1. **Total budget limit** (cannot exceed \$760,000):

\[
x + y \le 760{,}000
\]

2. **Minimum proportion in condos** (at least 20% of *total invested* must be in condos):

\[
x \;\ge\; 0.2(x + y)
\]

This can also be rewritten (if you want it simplified) as:

\[
0.8x \ge 0.2y \quad \Leftrightarrow \quad 4x \ge y
\]

3. **Minimum amount in detached houses** (at least \$20,000):

\[
y \ge 20{,}000
\]

4. **Nonnegativity**:

\[
x \ge 0,\quad y \ge 0
\]

**Complete LP model**

\[
\begin{aligned}
\max\;& Z = 0.5x + y \\
\text{s.t. } 
& x + y \le 760{,}000, \\
& x \ge 0.2(x + y), \\
& y \ge 20{,}000, \\
& x \ge 0,\; y \ge 0.
\end{aligned}
\]

Optionally, using the simplified condo-share constraint \(4x \ge y\):

\[
\begin{aligned}
\max\;& Z = 0.5x + y \\
\text{s.t. } 
& x + y \le 760{,}000, \\
& 4x \ge y, \\
& y \ge 20{,}000, \\
& x \ge 0,\; y \ge 0.
\end{aligned}
\]

**4. Suitable Python libraries**

To solve this LP numerically in Python, good choices include:

- `PuLP` – simple syntax for LPs, integrates with CBC and other solvers.
- `scipy.optimize.linprog` – suitable for small/medium LPs, part of SciPy.
- `ortools.linear_solver` (Google OR-Tools) – industrial-strength solver interface.
- `cvxpy` – more general convex-optimization modeling language that also handles LPs.

import pulp

def main():
    # Create the LP problem
    prob = pulp.LpProblem("Real_Estate_Investment", pulp.LpMaximize)

    # Decision variables: dollars invested in condos (x) and detached houses (y)
    x = pulp.LpVariable('x', lowBound=0)
    y = pulp.LpVariable('y', lowBound=0)

    # Objective function: Maximize 0.5x + y
    prob += 0.5 * x + 1.0 * y

    # Constraints
    # 1) Total budget limit: x + y <= 760000
    prob += x + y <= 760000

    # 2) Minimum proportion in condos: x >= 0.2(x + y)  ->  4x >= y
    prob += 4 * x >= y

    # 3) Minimum amount in detached houses: y >= 20000
    prob += y >= 20000

    # Solve the LP
    prob.solve(pulp.PULP_CBC_CMD(msg=False))

    # Get objective value
    final_obj = pulp.value(prob.objective)

    print(f"FINAL_OBJ: {final_obj}")

if __name__ == "__main__":
    main()
